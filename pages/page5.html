GAP, Nike, Lululemon, and LVMH (Louis Vuitton Moët Hennessy) have solidified their positions as iconic brands by understanding their audiences and adapting to changing times. GAP Inc. owns Old Navy, The Gap, Banana Republic, and Athleta, leveraging these distinct brands to target various customer demographics, including loyal patrons of their other retail branches. Nike collaborates with high-performance athletes to inspire and connect with sports enthusiasts worldwide. Lululemon has become synonymous with timeless athleisure, offering high-quality basics that stand the test of time. And LVMH continues to market its instantly recognizable logos and designs, cementing its place as a symbol of luxury and status.
